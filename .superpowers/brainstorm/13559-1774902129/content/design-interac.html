Design Section 4: Interac e-Transfer

The honest reality — and how we make it seamless anyway

The Reality of Interac on iOS

Canadian banks (TD, RBC, BMO, Scotiabank, CIBC) do not expose public deep links that pre-fill an e-Transfer amount. This is a bank limitation, not an app limitation — there's no official Interac SDK for third-party apps.

What we CAN do — and it's still really smooth:

The Settle Up Flow

1
Tap "Settle Up" on any balance
Shows: "You owe Raj $240.00"
2
Amount auto-copies to clipboard
Toast: "240.00 copied" — ready to paste in your bank app
3
Raj's Interac email/phone shown
Each user adds their Interac contact in Profile — displayed here for easy reference
4
"Open Bank App" button
User picks their bank from a list — we open it directly. Amount is on clipboard, ready to paste.
5
"I've sent it" → marked settled
Back in Chip In, one tap marks the balance as settled. Confetti. Done. 🎉
